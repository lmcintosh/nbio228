Syllabus:

Week 1 (GC + IW): 3/02

Course Introduction

Equations Are Your Friends - math as a second language.

Remember Calculus - derivatives, integrals, and what was the fundamental theorem again?

Week 2 (GC): 3/09

Differential Equations - first-order ordinary differential equations, exponential growth and decay

Logarithms and Exponentials - why slide rules work, Weber's law

Introduction to Linear Algebra - basics of matrixes, N-dimensional spaces, linear independence

Week 3 (GC + IW): 3/16

More Linear Algebra - basis sets, linear transformations, eigenvectors

Introduction to Fourier Analysis - intro to frequency space, Fourier transforms, spectra

Week 4 (IW): 3/23

Applications of Fourier Transforms - linear systems, convolution, filtering, cross-correlation

Week 5 (IW): 3/30

Introduction to Probability - randomness, probability distributions, marginals, means, and modes

Week 6 (IW): 4/07

Statistics - variance, standard error, confidence intervals, significance, T-tests, ANOVA

Week 7 (GC): 4/14

Bayesian Inference - priors and posteriors, likelihoods, expectation, Bayesian networks

Week 8 (IW): 4/21

Analysis - PCA, classification, maximum likelihood, signal detection, ROC, clustering

Week 9 (GC): 4/28

Information Theory - Shannon, bits, and how to win at 20-questions
